Abuja Art International Present 2023 Annual Dance Festival.

Title: Our culture.

Dance is a way to let go, express yourself and connect with others. Every culture has its own unique style of dance, so join us in celebrating the artistry and beauty of dance.

2023.02.23(THU) ~ 26(SAT)

@ National Arts Theatre, Maitama, Abuja.

Programs

Lecture

Listen to the speakers from various countries about the messeges of sharing and opening.

Dance

Enjoy great Dance from top rated African dancers as they Dance and vibe to the beautiful sounds.

Drama

Watch the drama presentation from the best actors and actresses in the industry, as they explain the history of Dance.

Network

Connect with lots of like minds and peers in this exclusive event, meet industry experts and associates.

Experience

Have a funfilled experience. There is no better way to close the year than to celebrate your wins.
